These files are copied automatically into the ECU-TEST Workspace by the ECU-TEST MATLAB Interface.
These functions will be used for automatic ECU-TEST artifact generation.
During the automatic artifact generation, these functions will be called.
They can be removed or modified to meet the user-specific needs. 

Refer also to the ECU-TEST Help, Chapter "MLSIMULINK".
Type "help ecuTest.api.EcuTestController" into the matlab command line for further information.
